Our Philosophy

Lets be honest! All of us have been through the pain of finding a reference-table plumber or serviceman at one point or another.

And while we have all the technology and social networks in place, it is still very difficult to find get someone with reference. Yes, we have LinkedIn, and Facebook, and JustDial (yellow pages), but it is always that trusted friend who comes to the rescue. It is always someone that we or our friends know that we trust the most.

CLinkR was born with that philosophy.
CLinkR (Connection Linker) provides ability to reference a person via a trusted network. All transactions and interactions in the real world happen with reference. We are always looking for a way to validate a person, via a common connection, before doing any kind of engagement with the person.

CLinkR helps you find those shared connections that exist in the real world but are missing in the online world. From finding a plumber, to getting the number of the person you met at a party. From getting common reference for the interior decorator you are about to hire, to finding references for the bridegroom. References are used everywhere. CLinkR helps you find those shared connections. You can then call or text your common connection to get reference.
CLinkR also helps you find and discover people. Search for everyday services that you need. Your social circle most like has the number! Discover who has it and then ask him for the number. No more hassle of calling 100s of people to find out that one person who actually has the connection.

ClinkR helps you find and get references for these everyday needs.

Clinkr. Find. Refer. Connect. With friends, relatives and services.
